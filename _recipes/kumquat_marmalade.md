---
title: Kumquat Marmalade
---

## Source

https://lemonsandanchovies.com/2019/04/kumquat-marmalade/

## Notes

Adapted from source with modified ratios.

## Ingredients:

- 1.75 lb kumquats (808g), this is flexible
- 1.5 cup sugar
- 1.5 cup water
- 1 lemon, juiced

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare kumquats:**

   - Cut kumquats in half.
   - Squeeze seeds and juice through a strainer (reserve the juice).
   - Chop up the kumquat halves.

2. **Cook:**

   - Combine kumquats, reserved juice, sugar, water, and lemon juice in a pot.
   - Bring to a boil.
   - Keep boiling (without boiling over, that's an 8 on my induction stove-top) and simmer for 15 minutes.
