---
title: Tom Kha Gai
---

## Source

<https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/tom-ka-gai/>

## Ingredients:

- 3 cups unsalted chicken stock (or water + chicken boulion + hondashi)
- 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thigh, cut into 1-inch pieces
(Optional)
(Veggies that cook for longer)
- Carrot
- Napa Cabbage

- 1 13.5 fl oz can coconut milk

- 1 teabag lemongrass
- 12 thin slices galangal
- 6 makrut lime leaves, twisted to bruise and torn into big chunks

- 2 Thai chilies, smashed until broken or cut into large pieces (to taste)
- 2 Tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tsp brown sugar

(Veggies that can cook in 5 minutes)
- Mushrooms
- Onion
- Yellow squash

- 2½ Tbsp lime juice

- Chopped green onion and/or cilantro, for garnish
- Jasmine rice, for serving (optional)

## Instructions:

1. Bring chicken stock to a boil, add salt, chicken thigh, veggies that take time to cook (Napa, Carrot, etc). Simmer gently for 15 minutes or until chicken is fork tender.
2. Add coconut milk, lemongrass, galangal, makrut lime leaves, chilies, mushrooms / veggies that cook fast, about half the fish sauce, and sugar. Simmer 5 minutes to infuse.
3. Taste and add more fish sauce as needed. Turn off heat, add about 2 Tbsp lime juice, then taste and add more as needed. Top with green onions and/or cilantro.
4. Serve with jasmine rice. (Herbs are for infusion only — remove before serving or warn guests.)
