## Source

<https://storybites.co.nz/post/abc-soup>

## Ingredients:

- Pork ribs or Chicken w/Bone

Some combo of veggies:
- ★ Gold potatoes
- ★ Carrots
- ★ Tomatoes
- ★ Corn
- ★ Onions
- Tofu
- Mushrooms
- Zucchini
- Napa cabbage 

### Seasonings (to taste):
- Sugar
- Fish sauce

## Instructions:

1. (optional, depends on whether meat has lots of scum) Boil pork or chicken until cooked. Rinse under running water until water runs clear (removes scum).
2. Fill a stock pot with water and bring to a boil. Add all ingredients and simmer ~1.5 hours.
4. Season to taste.
