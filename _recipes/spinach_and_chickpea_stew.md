---
title: Spinach and Chickpea Stew
---

## Source

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1DWB1EOLw_s>

## Ingredients:

- 1/4 to 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- 1 (28 oz) can whole tomatoes
- 1 small knob ginger, sliced into coins
- 6 oz spinach (or kale / collard greens)
- 1 can chickpeas, with liquid
- Smoked paprika (sweet or hot)
- Salt and MSG
- 2 bay leaves
- Splash of sherry vinegar (or cider / white wine vinegar)
- Crusty bread, for serving

## Instructions:

1. Puree half the canned tomatoes with most of their juice and the ginger. Roughly chop the remaining tomatoes.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot. Sweat onions and garlic until softened.
3. Season with salt, MSG, and smoked paprika.
4. Add tomato-ginger puree, then spinach. Let wilt down.
5. Stir in chickpeas with their liquid, chopped tomatoes, and bay leaves.
6. Simmer uncovered 30-45 minutes.
7. Adjust seasoning. Stir in a splash of vinegar. Drizzle with good olive oil before serving with bread.
