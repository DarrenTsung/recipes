# Baked Salmon

## Ingredients:

- BBQ salmon slices from Trader Joe's

Curing Mix:

- 4 parts salt (80g)
- 1 part sugar (20g)

Combine in a jar and stir to mix.

## Instructions:

1. Sprinkle curing mix over salmon and let sit for 7 minutes.
2. Wash cure off salmon.
3. Broil skin-side up at 400F until internal temperature reaches 110F. Carry-over temp should reach 130F.
